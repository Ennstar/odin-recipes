Lasagna Recipe

Description

Simple yet easy recipe on how to make a meat lasagna.

This legendary recipe was created by John Chandler, who unfortunately unexpectedly passed away. Here, we are here to honor his legacy.

Ingredients

  • Sausage
  • Ground Beef
  • Garlic, Crushed
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Crushed Tomato Sauce
    •

    Steps

    1. Combine all meat with onion and garlic and cook until browned.
    2. Stir in sauce and simmer.
    3. While sauce is simmering, boil large pot of salt. Cook lasagna for 8 to 10 minutes and drain.
    4. Pre-heat oven.
    5. Assemble lasagna with simmered sauce and noodles. Layer as desired.
    6. Cook for 25 minutes or until brown. Cool before serving.
