Potato Soup

Description

High effort but high reward. This recipe will make you whole.

Ingredients

  • Bacon, Chopped
  • Celery, Diced
  • Onion, Chopped
  • Garlic, Minced
  • Potatoes, Peeled & Cubed
  • Chicken Stock
  • Butter, Flour, Cream

Steps

  1. Cook bacon until browned. Remove and drain bacon until 1/4 cup bacon grease remains.
  2. Cook celery and onion until translucent. Stir in rest of ingredients and cover with chicken stock. Simmer until potatoes are tender.
  3. Transfer to blender in 2 increments and puree.
