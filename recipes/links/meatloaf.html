Homely Meatloaf

Description

This simple meatload recipe will relive your inner childhood...good or bad.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Egg
  • Onion, Chopped
  • Milk
  • Dried Bread Crumbs
  • Ketchup
  • Brown Sugar
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350F or 175C. Lightly grease pan.
  2. Combine and mix beef, egg, onion, milk, bread crumbs, and egg in large bowl. Transfer to greased pan.
  3. Mix remaining ingredients and pour over meatloaf and spread evenly.
  4. Bake for 1 hour or until no longer pink in center.
